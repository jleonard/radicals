What are radicals?

Radicals are a prototyping tool & teaching method for crafting user interfaces. In the contect of a UI, radicals are granular patterns of UI functionality.

Why “radicals”?

Radicals in Japanese

In the Japanese alphabet, a radical is a common sub-element found across different characters. Every character has a radical and radicals can be characters on their own.

Comparing Radicals & Components

In the context of a UI, radicals are the granular pieces of UI functionality. Each can stand on its own or be combined with other radicals to create new forms of interaction.
